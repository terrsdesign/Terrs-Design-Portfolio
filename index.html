My approach

01. Research

I explore the brand, the product, the users and the goals behind the project. Before diving into the design phase I need to understand the client and define the problem.

02. Strategy

I turn the insights into a clear direction, defining priorities, key steps and the best approach to keep the project going.

03. Design

I translate my strategy into a visual direction, building the first wireframes and working on a design system. I always keep the user at the center of the design process and ensure the solution is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

04. Prototype

I turn the design into an interactive prototype that can be tested with real users, to get real feedback and iterate on the solution.

05. Refine

I review the results, improve what needs to be adjusted and add the final details before delivery. Everything must feel intentional and well-crafted.

Is it always linear?

Not really — we can go back if we need to.
